Team Zak Attack's Tasks
8/8/23 - Team Allison and Nahum Zak
  1. This day is a godSand
    Pack your bags and leave your AirBNB for good. Per our discussion, and your choice, you are headed to the Great Sand Dunes National Park where you will see the tallest Dunes in the Americas.
    With regard to timing this is your day to carve out. Dogs are allowed on a leash. Your pass allows you 24 hour entry. Sunscreen is a must and you will need to submit 10 pictures. Night time star gazing is recommended. There are few places on Earth high enough above the light pollution of the cities that see a sky like this.
    Extra points if the two of you are uniquely (you choose the style) dressed in any of the photos.
    Important Notes: The sand reaches 150 degrees in the height of the day so you would be advised to hike on sand either early or later in the day. The trails and lakes should be lovely all day for you. The weather can vary however so wearing multiple layers, that can be removed, is advised. Bring plenty of food and water. Also bring doggy shoes if you have them.
    You will receive points for photos of:
    • Postcard Photos
    • Starry Nights (Sunset is 8:05 PM)
    • Sand Dunes (higher the better)
    • Wild Life (1 per species)
    • Waterfalls
    • Sand Sledding (Especially if you’re on the sled)
    Please note that bonus activities posted for you may be opened even if you have not completed Task #1
  2. If you have extra time, you can get points for photos of:
    • Bishop Castle in Rye 12705 CO-165. This has to be seen to be believed.
    • Big Head MLK and Emmit Till Statue in Pueblo.
    • Any of the sites from previous days that you may have missed.
8/7/23 - Team Allison and Nahum Zak estimated total time 8 hrs (All events are flexible unless ticketed).
  1. Lions and Tigers and Bear or Heights and Boulders and Air?
    You must either:
    • Proceed to the Wild Animal Sanctuary in Keensburg where you will learn to handle Tigers, deadly snakes and other animals and walk the highest elevated walkway in North America. Your guide Danny will have details for you.
      • OR...
    • Drive the highway to the sky 12,000 feet as you see the wildlife at Conde Naste Traveler’s top ten Park in the World Rocky Mountain National Park.
    Please note:
    • You have a pass in the app that only allows you to enter during a 12-2 window.
    • You are allowed to stay as long as you like.The park is open 24/7.
    • Dogs are only allowed in the vehicle, in picnic areas, campgrounds and on park roads but not on hiking trails.
  2. Click here to find nearby hikes if desired: https://www.nps.gov/romo/planyourvisit/pets.htm
    Drive carefully (seriously) on the “Highway to the Sky,” on Trail Ridge Road. This is the highest continuous paved road in the United states at over 12,000 ft!
    • Bonus: Take Old Fall River Road to the Apline visitor center on your way to Trail Ridge.
    • Note: The Overlooks are amazing. My personal favorite is the Forest Canyon Overlook.
  3. You will receive points for photos of:
    • Wildlife (only 1 submission per animal)
    • Postcard Photos
    • A surrounding town (Grand Lake for example)
    • A picnic area (Bear Lake or Sprague Lake for example)
    • A photo of the two of you dressed for a wedding while outdoors
    Be sure to check the site as bonus tasks will be posted later in the day.
    Their addresses are below!:
    • 1201 9th st Boulder
    • 1619 Pine St Boulder
    • Boulder Canyon Drive (South Side of Highway 119)
    • Dallas Street in Aurora South Side of US 40E Colfax Ave Crosstreet
  4. Bonus points for photos of:
    • The “Unglued 16 foot blue 3 legged chair in Aurora
    • The home where Mork from Ork stayed while in Boulder
    • El Vado Motel Junk Sculptures
    • The famed Lollipop Grave in Columbia Cemetery
    • A VIDEO of one or both of you in a dance off with a stranger while wearing a souvenier (t-shirt, hat, etc...) from your location
  5. As always remember:
    • Continue keeping a journal of notes for us to be presented on Shabbos.
    • Send only a single e-mail when each day is completed with your ten photos as well as start and finish times of each task.
    • Do not open your next clue until the task at hand is complete even if it appears early.
    • Note that not all teams are running on the same days. We will continue updating on August 7th and list each team’s day 2 scores regardless of when they were run.
    • Submit your daily recap e-mail as early as possible.
8/6/23 - Team Allison and Nahum Zak estimated total time 7 hrs (All events are flexible unless ticketed).
  1. A Godly High (8:00)
    Your mission is to locate and proceed to the Garden where Zeus, Hera, Hades and Poseidon might have played as children before overthrowing Cronus.
    Extra points for photographing any Kissing Camels or Balanced Rocks that you might find. Remember you should personally be in the photo when able and only your best 10 photos can be submitted in a day.
    Please:
    • Begin keeping a journal of notes for us to be presented on Shabbos.
    • Send only a single e-mail when each day is completed with your ten photos as well as start and finish times of each task.
    • Do not open your next clue until the task at hand is complete even if it appears early.
    • Note that not all teams are running on the same days. We will begin updating on August 6th and list each team’s day 1 scores regardless of when they were run.
    • Submit your daily recap e-mail as early as possible.
  2. Ride or High with your Ride or Die (12:00)
    Are you a daredevil or do you prefer to watch? You must choose between either an instruction course followed by a tandem skydive at Colorado Mountain High or getting roped into an incrediBULL experience?
    • Skydiving instructions will be provided by your instructor Dianne.
    • IncrediBULL experience is in your ticketmaster account at the Douglas County Fairgrounds. Your mission is to photograph:
      • A rider on a bull
      • A group of people unironically dressed in cowboy boots and hats (extra points for a photo of the two of you dressed for the occasion)!
  3. The rest of the day is yours but opportunity still abounds. Bonus points available for any of the following. Remember you can only submit 10 pictures.
    • Big Foot Crossing sign
    • 20 foot high hammer made of recycled metal
    • Random Humpty Dumpty statues
    • Truffles - A Pig statue that cannot fly
    • Pumpkin Man Statue
    • 11 ft Pumpkin
    Their addresses are below but we forgot which one belongs to which site! (All in Colorado Springs):
    • 5069 Pike’s Peak Hwy
    • 1100 South Royer St
    • 100 E Colorado Ave
    • 102 S Tejon St
    • 215 S Tejon St
    • 106 Pueblo Ave
